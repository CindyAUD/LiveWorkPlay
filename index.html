SKY GARDEN

Hello and welcome to Sky Garden.
A blend of nature-inspired design and contemporary living
to create a world that captivates minds, bodies, and souls
is the vision for this holistic resort development on 19 acres.
In an embodiment of truly interconnected living,
the magnificent Botanical Garden on one side is intertwined
with lively Urban Square at the other through the Residence square,
shopping mall, leisure center, and commercial spaces,
all being carefully arranged in such a way
as to keep you moving from first light to sunset.

For the spirit of Turi kumwe-Turumvirana

The elegant park invites residents and the sun to linger by the waters.
With a leisurely sip in a casual yet refined setting, relive the nostalgia of warm summer days.
Allow the evening to unfold in the company of friends, with an impressive menu
and five-star service to enhance your experience.
An entertainment luminary comes to life at the core of this architectural marvel.
Residents benefit from a variety of amenities of spas, lounges, work zones,
fitness facilities, and audiovisual delights. Residents can walk directly into the commercial center,
which offers a plethora of retail, dining, and socializing opportunities.
